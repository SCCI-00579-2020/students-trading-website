Welcome to TUK Trade Circle

Are you ready to save big this semester?

What is TUK Trade Circle?
TTC is a platform that allows you to buy or sell your items such as art and craft,devices, textbooks and other essential items such as calculators, clickers, and more from students in Technical University of Kenya.

Why should I use it?
Things are way cheaper when bought through this website. On average, you can save up to 65% on books by using the platform. If you are a seller, you get cash for your old items. There is no need to keep a huge pile of old things you're not using anymore . By selling, you contribute in helping thousands of students who cannot afford new things.
